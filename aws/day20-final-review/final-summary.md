## Study Tips

✅ Understand concepts, not just memorize
✅ Know service limitations and constraints
✅ Learn when to use each service
✅ Understand trade-offs between domains
✅ Practice with realistic scenarios
✅ Read questions twice carefully
✅ Eliminate obviously wrong answers
✅ Think about real-world use cases

## Common Exam Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Only focusing on one domain
❌ Choosing most expensive option
❌ Ignoring security requirements
❌ Not reading questions fully
❌ Overcomplicating designs
❌ Assuming always need Multi-AZ
❌ Ignoring cost considerations
